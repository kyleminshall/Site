Join the elements and get to the tile with 256 isotopic mass! Watch out for unstable elements though, as these won't be around forever!

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Elements are: H - Hydrogen, He - Helium, Li - Lithium, O - Oxygen, P - Phosphorus, Cu - Copper, I - Iodine, Fm - Fermium, Re - Reglium, Vb - Vibranium, Ad - Adamantium, Ub - Unobtanium
